The Alfaizam Palette

Aged Agarwood

Sourced from the deepest forests of Southeast Asia and aged for a minimum of 10 years to develop its rich, smoky complexity.

Taif Rose

Hand-picked at dawn in the mountains of Saudi Arabia, bringing a velvety, opulent floral heart to our signature blends.

Ambergris

A rare oceanic treasure that imparts an unparalleled warmth and longevity, fixing the fragrance to the skin for over 24 hours.

Indian Sandalwood

Creamy, milky sandalwood sustainably harvested from Mysore, India — the world's finest source for this precious base note.

Persian Saffron

The most precious spice on earth, adding a warm, honey-like radiance and golden richness to our most exclusive compositions.

White Musk

A clean, skin-close musk that acts as the invisible thread tying every composition together, lasting beautifully for 12+ hours.

Ancient Traditions

The Art of Scent Layering

In Arabian perfumery, a fragrance is not merely worn; it is built. The ancient art of layering allows you to create a bespoke olfactory signature that is entirely and uniquely your own.

Begin with a rich base of our concentrated Attar oils applied to pulse points. Allow it to melt into your skin before misting an enveloping cloud of Eau de Parfum. The result is a multi-dimensional aura that evolves dynamically and lingers for over 24 hours.
