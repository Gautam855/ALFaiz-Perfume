Frequently Asked Questions

WE ARE HERE TO ASSIST YOU

What is the difference between Perfume and Attar?
Attars are highly concentrated fragrance oils produced through traditional distillation, containing no alcohol. Perfumes (Eau de Parfum) are alcohol-based sprays with a lighter projection but varied complexity.
How long do your fragrances last?
Our Extrait de Parfum and Pure Oils are designed for maximum longevity. On average, they last between 8 to 14 hours depending on the skin type and environmental conditions.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, Alfaizam ships to over 150 countries. Shipping times vary from 3-7 business days for express delivery.
How should I store my perfume?
To preserve the integrity of the notes, store your bottles in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight and extreme temperature fluctuations.
